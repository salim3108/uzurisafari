The Northern Circuit is Tanzania's most famous safari region, perfect for first-time visitors and seasoned explorers alike. Here, you'll find the country's most celebrated parks:

Serengeti National Park: Witness the awe-inspiring Great Migration, where millions of wildebeest and zebras thunder across endless plains under the watchful eyes of lions and cheetahs.

Ngorongoro Crater: Explore the world's largest intact volcanic caldera, teeming with wildlife including elephants, rhinos, and flamingos.

Tarangire National Park: Known for its massive elephant herds and iconic baobab trees, Tarangire is a hidden gem with fewer crowds.

üêò Southern Tanzania - Untouched Wilderness and Secluded Adventures

For those seeking a more remote and authentic safari experience, Southern Tanzania offers vast, wild landscapes and fewer tourists. Here, you'll discover:

Ruaha National Park: Tanzania's largest national park, home to immense elephant and buffalo herds, towering baobabs, and excellent predator sightings.

Nyerere National Park (Selous Game Reserve): Cruise along the Rufiji River among hippos and crocodiles, or track wild dogs and lions in this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Mikumi National Park: A hidden gem with sweeping grasslands reminiscent of the Serengeti, perfect for spotting giraffes, zebras, and large herds of antelope.

Southern Tanzania offers authentic, off-the-beaten-path safaris for those seeking solitude and a deeper connection with nature.

ü¶ç Western Tanzania - Remote Frontiers and Primate Encounters

For the adventurous at heart, Western Tanzania is a remote frontier where few travelers venture. Here, you can experience:

Katavi National Park: Discover one of Africa's least-visited parks, where vast herds of buffalo and elephants gather, and crocodiles line the drying riverbeds.

Mahale Mountains National Park: Trek through lush forests to find wild chimpanzees in their natural habitat, surrounded by stunning views of Lake Tanganyika.

Gombe Stream National Park: Famous for Jane Goodall's groundbreaking chimpanzee research, Gombe offers intimate encounters with our closest relatives in a breathtaking setting.

Western Tanzania is for those who crave true wilderness and the thrill of discovering Africa's hidden gems.
