Our Story

Uzuri Safaris Ltd was founded in 2020, we believe travel should be personal, meaningful, and unforgettable. Based in the heart of Tanzania — one of East Africa's most stunning and diverse destinations we specialize in tailor-made safaris that go far beyond the ordinary.

Guided by our core values — authenticity, personal connection, and community empowerment we craft experiences that are as meaningful as they are magical.

With our own fleet, expert local guides, and custom itineraries, we offer exclusive safaris that connect you deeply with nature, culture, and community.

This isn't just adventure.
This is your journey of a lifetime — the Uzuri way.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Mission

At Uzuri Safaris Ltd., our mission is to create exceptional, tailor-made travel experiences that showcase the true beauty of Tanzania. We are committed to offering authentic adventures, personalized service, and promoting responsible tourism that uplifts local communities and preserves our natural heritage.

Our Vision

To be Tanzania's most trusted and inspiring specialized tour company, known for providing memorable and impactful adventures that bridge tourists with Africa's rich landscapes, vibrant cultures, and timeless spirit. Our vision is to leave a positive legacy empowering local people and preserving the natural wonders that make every journey extraordinary.
